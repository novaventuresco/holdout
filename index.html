The science

What's happening when a craving hits is mostly neurochemical. Dopamine spikes in anticipation of reward. That's the pull you feel. But it doesn't hold that level. It drops, usually within 15 to 20 minutes, whether you act on it or not.

That's what the 20-minute window is based on. If you can get past the peak, the craving doesn't disappear, but it gets smaller. Most people never see that happen because they give in before the peak passes.
