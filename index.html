The science

Habit-driven urges run on dopamine. Dopamine doesn't keep building forever — the impulse peaks and falls on its own, usually within 15 to 20 minutes, whether you act on it or not.

Most people give in before they see that happen. The app makes the window visible.

Questions

A few things
people ask.

Is it free?

Yes. The app is free to download and use. There are nine visual themes — three come with the app. The remaining six are a one-time $4.99 unlock if you want them. No subscription, nothing recurring.

Is my data private?

Nothing leaves your phone. Session records and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no third parties, and no network requests. The app works entirely offline.

What kind of cravings is this for?

The mechanic is general — it does not target any specific behavior. People use it for food cravings, shopping impulses, and similar urge-driven moments where a 20-minute gap is all they need. It is not a medical product and does not treat any condition.

Does it work offline?

Entirely. There is no login, no sync, no network required. You open it, run the session, and everything stays on your device.
